# Pivot

**Pivot** is a simple iOS app that helps you manage your time — specifically, balancing the stuff you *want* to do with the stuff you *should* be doing.

The idea is pretty straightforward. It’s totally fine to play games, watch videos, or scroll for a bit. But at some point, it’s time to pivot. This app helps with that.

## How it works

You set a timer when you’re doing something fun, like gaming or watching YouTube. Maybe you’ve given yourself 30 minutes a day for it. When the time’s up, Pivot gives you a nudge — hey, maybe time to switch to something a little more meaningful.

You can track how much time you're spending on both types of tasks. It’s not about guilt, just awareness.

## Why use it

We all waste time sometimes. Pivot doesn’t try to stop that — it just helps make sure it doesn’t take over your whole day. It’s about balance, not restriction.

## What it does

- Lets you set daily, weekly, or monthly limits for “want to” tasks
- Starts timers for those activities
- Reminds you to switch to “should do” tasks when time’s up
- Tracks how much time you're spending on each type
